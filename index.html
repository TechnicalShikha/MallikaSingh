Mallika Singh

Mallika Singh is an Indian television actress. She is famous for her portrayal of the role of ‘Radha‘ in Star Bharat’s Hindu mythological serial ‘RadhaKrishn‘ (2018). Let’s check out more about Mallika Singh, her personal life, career, and other interesting facts.

Biography/Wiki

Mallika Singh was born on 15 September 2000 (18 years; as in 2018) in Jammu, Jammu & Kashmir, India. She made her TV debut with the Star Bharat’s mythological drama serial ‘RadhaKrishn’ (2018). Mallika is portraying the role of ‘Radha‘ in the serial. The show is based on the epic love saga of Lord Krishna and Radha. Although she is from Jammu, she came to Mumbai, Maharashtra to visit her maternal aunt but got selected for the role of Radha opposite Sumedh Mudgalkar (playing the role of Krishna). This proved to be the turning point in her life.

Physical Appearance

Mallika Singh stands tall at 5’ 4” and weighs around 50 kg. Her figure measurements are 32-26-32. Her eyes are brown in color and her hair is black.

Family & Religion

Mallika Singh was born in a Hindu family. She has one younger sister. Her mother, Ruby Singh, is a choreographer and a classical dance teacher in Jammu. She is unmarried as of now. Mallika Singh faced difficulties when she shifted to Mumbai because of the different lifestyle than Jammu. She used to cry a lot but her mother kept her busy with dancing and audition. Mallika has been residing in Mumbai, Maharashtra for the for the past two and a half years, along with her maternal grandparents.

Career

Mallika Singh completed her schooling from Seven Square Academy School. According to her, she was never interested in acting; and it was only after her mother’s insistence that she gave the auditions for the role of ‘Radha.’ Mallika was in 10th standard when she gave the auditions. After a long gap of two and a half years, the producer of Star Bharat’s ‘RadhaKrishn’, Sidharth Kumar, informed her that she was selected. She also said that she got the role of ‘Radha’ because of her innocent persona. Although she remains busy shooting for the show, she also mentions that she never leaves her study behind.
